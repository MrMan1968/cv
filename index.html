Simon Hammond

Locum Optometrist with a passion for Meteorology, and Founder of WessexWeather.

After growing up in the Midlands I embarked on my career in optometry in South Wales. I have various hobbies and interests including photography and astronomy, but my passion is meteorology, with a particular interest in clouds and Climate Change. I have been recording and observing the weather since around the age of ten, and now run the WessexWeather website which contains a wealth of information about weather and climate in Southern England.
